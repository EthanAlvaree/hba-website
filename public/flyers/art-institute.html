Most art classes teach in a vacuum. We don’t.

Students learn the same tools and workflows used by professionals shipping hardcovers today — Clip Studio Paint, layered PSDs, non-destructive workflows, flatting, holds, rendering, lettering — and they apply them to pages that get printed and bound.

Each PCI student’s flatting, rendering, and lighting work appears on real pages in real books. They earn a complimentary copy of the hardcover and their name in the credits page, alongside the working professionals on the team.

We don’t use AI prompts. We don’t teach shortcuts. We teach why every line and every color matters.
